Applications


If you'd like to apply for the University of Guelph Robotics team: fill out the application below and then email it to ugrt@uoguelph.ca.

After we've reviewed your application, we'll call you in for a face to face interview to get to know you and see how well you will work with the team. You don't necessarily need to know everything about robitics to be a great member of the team, just a willingness to work and learn.
